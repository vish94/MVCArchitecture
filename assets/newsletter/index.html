EXCELSIOR 2015

SRDTU Presents Excelsior 2015

It is my pleasure to present to you Excelsior (Society of Robotics- Delhi Technological University fest) to be held from 6th to 8th of February, 2015 at DTU (Delhi Technological University), Delhi. Over the years, fest organized by Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) have attracted myriads of college students from various parts of the India. Furthermore, Excelsior carried on the juggernaut with its inaugural edition at DTU last year. SR-DTU is a pivotal and synergistic organizer of fest at DTU. Now, with this edition, SR-DTU, consolidating on last year’s experience, envisages this technical extravaganza to reach an unforeseen level in India. Buoyed by superlative participation figures, avant-garde designs and unwavering intent of organizing team, SR-DTU is optimistic to bolster the legacy of Excelsior. This immensely productive and popular event acts as a platform for students from various parts of India to showcase their technological and engineering prowess by engineering and realizing various challenges as per the given requirements. The event is also a medium to promote out of box thinking and requires the participants to use their lateral thinking and bring in unique perspective. Excelsior involves designing and fabrication of robots along with several other competition for students where teams have to conceptualize and fabricate a robot/project to participate in a three day event comprising of various categories of events. The objective of this fest is to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate their analytic and logical skills.
